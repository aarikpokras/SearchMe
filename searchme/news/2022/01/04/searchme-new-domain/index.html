SearchMe moving to SearchMe.one






Home

    For SearchMe's entire life, it has had a domain - searchmebrowser.com - that forwards to searchme.glitch.me. SearchMe will not be renewing this domain because it is long, hard to remember, and does not reflect the website's actual purpose.
    As of Sunday, January 9, 2022, SearchMe will be moving to searchme.one. This domain is far more secure than searchmebrowser.com and easier to remember.
    The email addresses that are listed under searchmebrowser.com will be transferred to @searchme.one. This should be a seamless transfer and should not affect service in any way.
    After January 9, you will no longer be able to access SearchMe through searchmebrowser.com
